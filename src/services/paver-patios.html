Pavers

Versatility and Value

Pavers are among the most popular materials for landscapes because they go well with almost any landscape theme and require very little maintenance. They come in a variety of shapes and sizes, and we can cut them to follow whatever contour you need for your design. Paver patios provide a smooth, cohesive look while also being slip-resistant. They also aren't prone to cracking over time like concrete would.

Types of Pavers

Pavers are generally divided into concrete, stone, and clay. Each has its own unique looks, benefits, and drawbacks such as natural look, flexibility, durability, price, and more. Our team is well-versed in the variety of paver types, so we can help you choose the perfect type for your design.

Cost

The cost of installing a paver patio can vary greatly depending upon the type of pavers and the contours of the area they are fitted to. Generally, you can expect to pay between $35 to $65 per square foot. Paver patios are more expensive than concrete, but are a more durable option that require less maintenance and typically have a greater return-on-investment for homeowners.
