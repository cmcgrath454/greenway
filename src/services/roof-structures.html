Roof Structures

Relax in the Shade

The Midwest climate can really limit the amount of time that you can comfortably spend outside each year. Adding a roof structure to your landscape is one of the solutions to take advantage of as much time in your outdoor living area as possible each year by providing protection from sun, rain, and snow.

Types

There are many different types of roof structures that can be added into a landscape. They can be made out of a variety of different materials to match the theme of your landscape, and can be attached or detached from existing structures like your house or a deck. Below are some examples of roof structures that are both useful and attractive.

  • Attached Roof Overhang
  • Screened Porch
  • Gazebo
  • Cabana
  • Sunroom
