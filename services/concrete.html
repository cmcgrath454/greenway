Concrete

Customization

Concrete is extremely versatile in landscaping projects, and with our knowledgeable team, can be shaped and colored perfectly to add customization to elements in your landscape. As an added bonus, concrete is extremely easy to clean and maintain to keep a fresh appearance. Concrete typically works best in the following landscape features, but can also be utilized in a variety of other manners.

Stamped Concrete

Stamped concrete allows for a large number of possibilities when it comes to patterns and colors. It can also be much more cost-effective than using natural stone or pavers. Concrete is a durable material that can last a long time before needing repairs. The most popular patterns are those that mimic the patterns found in natural stones such as slate, flagstone, and fieldstone.

Cost

Concrete cost depends upon the customization to the concrete, such as stamping patterns or staining colors. However, you can expect to spend somewhere between $8 to $12 per square foot for a basic concrete look, and $12 to $20 per square foot for more intricate designs and color variations.
