Designing a Deck

Building or replacing a deck is a big investment into your home, and it's important to use an experienced company with knowledge of the best materials for both durability, safety, and beauty. Our team has extensive experience building custom decks in Nebraska and Iowa, and we only work with the most effective materials from TimberTech. We can help you design a deck that's perfect for your lifestyle, whether it's hosting family cookouts or relaxing in the shade.

TimberTech

One of the most important aspects of deck building is durability, which is why we use TimberTech materials. They are proven to endure the elements while retaining the natural look of wood. Not only are these materials top quality, but they are more cost-effective for deck building than using and constantly re-treating wooden decks. Cost

The cost of building a new deck can vary depending on the design and materials. We estimate that on average, a new deck costs between $75 to $150 per square foot. Reliable sources estimate that a new deck can provide homeowners with approximately a 70-80% return on investment.
